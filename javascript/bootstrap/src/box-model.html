Margin and Padding

They have the "m" and "p" prefixes respectively.

Sizes can vary from "0 to 5" and auto.


  <div class="p-3">...</div>
abc

To be more specific, you can also add more prefixes.

  • t (top), b (bottom), y (both top and bottom)
  • s (left), e (right), x (both left and right)

  <div class="mt-4 ms-2">...</div>

Border

First, use the class "border" to define the sides. You can even be more specific (top, bottom, start, end). Or contrast sides by adding "0".


  <div class="border">...</div>

  <div class="border-start">
  ...
  </div>

  <div class="border border-bottom-0">
  ...
  </div>
