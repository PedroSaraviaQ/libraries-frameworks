Margin and Padding

They have the "m" and "p" prefixes respectively.

Sizes can vary from "0 to 5" and auto.


  <div class="p-3">...</div>
abc

To be more specific, you can also add more prefixes.

  • t (top), b (bottom), y (both top and bottom)
  • s (left), e (right), x (both left and right)

  <div class="mt-4 ms-2">...</div>

Border-radius

Additionally, you can modify the border-radius with the "rounded" class, without having to add borders. You can either:

  • Add the size of corners (from 0 to 5)
  • Define the sides (top, bottom, start, end)
  • Or add special borders (circle, pill)

  <div class="rounded border">...</div>

  <div class="rounded-end">...</div>

  <div class="rounded-4">...</div>
